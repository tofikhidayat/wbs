What is World Blockchain Summit?

World Blockchain Summit is a global series of elite gatherings that takes place around the world including Kenya, Russia, Canada, Singapore, Germany, India, UAE, Brazil and Saudi Arabia.

It connects global blockchain gurus and technology players in this space including emerging startups with regional businesses, governments, and IT leaders from across key industry verticals.

The Summit features inspirational, thought-provoking keynotes, panel discussions, tech talks, workshops, fire-side chats and a host of networking opportunities.
